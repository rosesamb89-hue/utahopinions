Welcome. This is the first post on **Utah Opinions** — a place to publish essays and commentary on Utah politics, education, and the issues that shape life in this state.

The plan is simple: long-form writing, posted when I have something worth saying, organized by topic so you can read what interests you and ignore what doesn't.

## What you'll find here

- **Politics** — what's happening at the Capitol, how it lands for the people it affects, what's being missed.
- **Education** — Utah's schools, teachers, students, and the policy fights that shape them.
- **Issues** — water, housing, growth, the pieces of public life that don't fit neatly anywhere else.
- **Commentary** — shorter takes, reactions, and notes.

## What this isn't

This isn't breaking news. There are good reporters in Utah doing that work. This is the slower version — context, analysis, and opinion.

If you'd like to follow along, the [RSS feed](/rss.xml) is the best way. More to come.
